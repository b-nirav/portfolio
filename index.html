    About Me

    Welcome to my portfolio website! I’m thrilled to share my journey with you. With a Master of Science in Mathematics, I began my professional career in the classroom, teaching Mathematics to graduate, undergraduate, and high school students. Those years taught me the power of curiosity, the joy of problem-solving, and the importance of communicating complex ideas in ways that resonate.

    As someone who’s always been fascinated by the intersection of math, technology, and real-world impact, I found myself drawn to data analytics. This transition wasn’t just a career shift—it was a natural evolution of my passion for uncovering patterns, solving challenges, and turning numbers into actionable stories. I love diving into data to uncover the "why" and craft impactful, data-driven solutions.

    In this portfolio, you’ll find projects that showcase not only my technical skills—like Python, SQL, and Tableau—but also my ability to approach problems with curiosity, creativity, and a results-oriented mindset. From predictive models to visualization dashboards, each project represents a step in my journey of learning and contributing through data.

    Thank you for taking the time to visit. I hope my work resonates with you, and I invite you to connect if you’re interested in collaboration or exploring how I can bring data-driven insights to your initiatives.
